What is Doodle Health?




The Doodle Health, founded in Feburary 2022, is an online counseling platform, catering to the unique mental health needs of students across the globe. We strive to create a safe space by allowing individuals to tell their story in a supportive & non-judgmental environment.Our Psychologists, Counselors & Therapists bring a diverse set of expertise, experience & skill sets to address concerns. Our platform is helping awareness among educational institutions to build a healthier interaction among the Student, the Counsellor and the institutes in building a mentally healthy atmosphere around your campus.

Things need to change.

And we are leading the way!
